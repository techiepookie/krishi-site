About This Project

Deep Fake Detector was created as a hackathon project aimed at addressing the growing concern of manipulated media online. With the rise of synthetic media technologies, it's becoming increasingly difficult to distinguish between authentic and artificial content.


Our team developed this tool using a combination of convolutional neural networks and frequency domain analysis to identify telltale signs of manipulation that might not be visible to the human eye. The project leverages TensorFlow.js to run complex ML models directly in the browser, making powerful deepfake detection accessible to everyone.


While still in development, our model currently achieves a 94% accuracy rate on standard deepfake detection benchmarks. We're continuously improving our algorithms and training on newer, more sophisticated deepfakes to stay ahead of advancing technology.
